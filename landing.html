Meet the Pastry Chef!


Meet Mayela, the heart and hands behind Knead Sweets.


As a passionate baker with a love for creating delicious and beautiful treats, Mayela has been perfecting the art of baking for years. Whether she's experimenting with new flavors or perfecting timeless classics, Mayela is always focused on creating baked goods that bring happiness to others.
